# Research Summary

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at The University of Queensland, researching the theoretical foundations of modern AI systems and methods for principled uncertainty quantification in deep learning. My work combines mathematical analysis and large-scale empirical evaluation to better understand and improve the reliability of machine learning systems.

**Research interests:** Bayesian deep learning, uncertainty quantification, random matrix theory, neural tangent kernels, statistical learning theory, and generalization in deep neural networks.
